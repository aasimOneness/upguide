Privacy Policy

When you sign up or purchase something from our site, as part of the buying and selling process, we collect the personal information you give us such as your name, mobile or telephone number and email address.

If you were to sign up to receive our posts by email, we collect your email address and mobile number. Our Email Service Provider manages the subscription and hence shall have access to the same as well. We may disclose your personal information if we are required by law to do so or if you violate our Terms of Service. We primarily use Razorpay for processing payments and they would require some of these information supplied to them in order process the order.

We do not store your card data on our servers but Razorpay may store a masked version of your credit card number. The data is encrypted through the Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard (PCI-DSS) when processing payment. Your purchase transaction data is only used as long as is necessary to complete your purchase transaction. For more insight, you may also want to read terms and conditions of razorpay on https://razorpay.com

Third Party Services

In general, the third-party providers used by us will only collect, use and disclose your information to the extent necessary to allow them to perform the services they provide to us. However, certain third-party service providers, such as payment gateways and other payment transaction processors, have their own privacy policies in respect to the information we are required to provide to them for your purchase-related transactions. For these providers, we recommend that you read their privacy policies so you can understand the manner in which your personal information will be handled by these providers.
