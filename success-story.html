How Upguide Works

The steps in our highly organised approach to helping our students gain admission to the best universities in the world include:

01. Crafting an initial, highly individualised plan

During your initial assessment with one of our expert academic advisors you will have the opportunity to both learn from the advisor's expertise and ask as many questions as you like. Once the advisor has a good idea as to your age, current academic situation and passions, they can craft a specialised plan and make recommendations as to the next steps in your UpGuide journey. Book Free Session

02. Meeting your Educational Coordinator

Your educo is your 'go to' for all things scheduling and organising. Dedicated to helping you organise your entire UpGuide journey, your educo will coordinate your tutor and mentor scheduling, assist you with actioning all action steps recommended by your UpGuide team, and act as a regular communication base for you and your parents or guardians.

Book Free Session
03. Planning with your UpGuide Strategist

Your strategist acts as the 'captain of your application plan ship', helping you build out a personalised university application list and formulate a game plan which includes the construction of a highly personalised roadmap. It is your strategist who will help you pull all elements of your application(s) together, ensuring your final submission(s) will be as perfect as they can be. Book Free Session

04. Working with your UpGuide team

UpGuide's unique algorithm matches students to their team based on their academic goals, passions and learning styles. Your team will include: tutors who are standardised test top scorers, extracurricular mentors whose profiles reflect their own international impact, essay mentors who gained admission to Harvard, Oxford and more, and interview experts who'll ensure you are interview confident/ready.
